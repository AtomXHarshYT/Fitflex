Weight Loss Diet Plan

@TrainBySunny

Daily Meal Schedule

Morning Wake-Up Drink

500 ml Water with Apple Cider Vinegar or Lemon (for digestion)

Breakfast

30g Oats or 1 Bowl Muesli (whole-grain cereal)
10 Almonds (rich in healthy fats)

Morning Snack

One Bowl of Papaya (for hydration and vitamins)

Lunch

100g Rice (preferably brown rice)
Dal (Lentil Soup)
Fresh Salad (with leafy greens and veggies)

Evening Snack

Green Tea (for metabolism boost)
Any Fruit (like apple or orange)
Mixed Dry Fruits (small handful for energy)

Dinner

100g Paneer (Indian cottage cheese) or 50g Soya Chunks (rich in protein)
100g Rice
Fresh Salad (with light dressing)

Tips for Success

  • Drink plenty of water throughout the day.
  • Engage in regular cardio and strength training exercises.
  • Avoid processed and sugary foods as much as possible.
"Discipline and consistency are the keys to weight loss success!"
