Weight Gain Diet Plan

@TrainBySunny

Daily Meal Schedule

Morning Wake-Up Drink

500 milliliters Water with Apple Cider Vinegar or Lemon (for digestion and hydration)

Breakfast

Oats Weight Gain Shake (with milk, peanut butter, banana, and honey)

Morning Snack

One Bowl of Papaya (hydrating and full of vitamins)

Lunch

250 grams Rice (energy-rich carbohydrates)
Dal (Lentil Soup, a great protein source)
Yogurt (Dahi, for probiotics)
Fresh Salad (including cucumbers, tomatoes, and greens)

Evening Snack

Two Slices of Bread with Peanut Butter (healthy fats and protein)
Two Bananas (quick energy boost)

Dinner

100 grams Paneer (Indian cottage cheese) or 50 grams Soya Chunks (rich in protein)
250 grams Rice (easy-to-digest carbs)
Fresh Salad (light and refreshing)

Tips for Success

  • Eat calorie-dense meals to support weight gain.
  • Incorporate strength training exercises to build muscle mass.
  • Stay consistent and gradually increase portion sizes as needed.
"Fuel your body, build your strength, and stay dedicated!"
