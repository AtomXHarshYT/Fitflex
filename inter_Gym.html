Intermediate Workout Routine

Weekly Workout Schedule

Monday - Chest

Flat Bench Press
Incline Press
Pec Dec Fly
Dumbbell Pullover
Chest Dips (3 sets of 8-10 reps)

Tuesday - Back

Push-ups
Lat Pulldown
T-Bar Row
Seated Row
Reverse Lat Pulldown
Single-Arm Dumbbell Row (3 sets of 10-12 reps per arm)

Wednesday - Shoulders

Shoulder Press
Side Raise
Front Raise
Shrug
Arnold Press (3 sets of 10-12 reps)

Thursday - Triceps

Rope Extensions
Overhead Extension
Rod Extension
Diamond Push-ups
Close-Grip Bench Press (3 sets of 8-10 reps)

Friday - Biceps

Barbell Curl
Hammer Curl
Dumbbell Curl
Preacher Curl
Concentration Curl (3 sets of 10-12 reps per arm)

Saturday - Legs

Squats
Leg Extension
Leg Curl
Calf Raises
Lunges (3 sets of 12 reps per leg)

Sunday - Rest

Take a full day of rest to recover.

Tips

Focus on your form to prevent injuries and maximize gains. Consistency is key to achieving your fitness goals.
